RECREATING 'SUPREMATISM' BY KASIMIR MALEVICH

Working out the layers, colours and their relationships to each other was very interesting. Getting the tadpole
right was near impossible. The original is below for comparison. It might have been easier to copy the Black Square.

It loses all the texture and character of the original, of course, but gains the opportunity to come to life again,
if only so that we can go back to the original and see it fresh. The exercise put me in mind of Matisse, whose
cut-outs would also make a good subject for this kind of study.
